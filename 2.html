Buwakan ni Alejandra

With Busay favorites Sirao Gardens and Florentino’s on everyone’s Instagram feed, add a new colorful attraction to the mix – Buwakan ni Alejandra! The vibrant flower garden is not to miss if you’re planning to explore the hidden gems of Busay. Translated in Bisaya as “Alejandra’s Flower Garden'', the landmark is home to 200 species of flowers that you’ll be able to see up close. Not only will you get to enjoy the season’s flowers, but also see the growing buds that are waiting to bloom. It can get quite breezy in the garden, so if you’re the type to get cold quite easily, don’t forget to pack a jacket!

  • Address: Transcentral Highway, Sitio Bunga Baas, Balamban, 6401 Cebu
  • Opening Hours: 7:00Am - 6:00PM, Daily
  • Entrance Fees: PHP 75, Child (Age 6-10)/Senior Citizen (Age 60+)/PWD- PHP 50
