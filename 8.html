21 Kilometers Coffee

A stop at Liel’s Kitchen is the perfect chance to see the beauty of Busay while enjoying the flavors of modern Japanese cuisine. Located within Serenity Farm and Resort, the restaurant is known for its stunning panoramic view of the mountainside, which you can admire while seated on their thrilling “hanging beds''. Don’t miss out on the chance to order their popular maki rolls and tempura ice cream as you enjoy the sights!


  • Address: Serenity Farm and Resort, Sitio Tiguib, Brgy Malubog 6000 Cebu City, Philippines
  • Opening Hours: 12:00PM-9:00PM, Daily
  • Entrance Fee: PHP 100 (Consumable, for both food and drinks)
