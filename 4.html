Situated around 2,000 feet above sea level, Top of Cebu is a recommended food stop when traveling up Busayâ€™s mountain. The scenic restaurant overlooking Cebu city, Mandaue, and Lapu-Lapu serves up delicious Filipino cuisine with a modern twist. You can definitely visit it at any time of day, whether the sun is shining bright over the skyline or stars are shimmering over Cebu at night. Top of Cebu is located near the popular Tops Lookout, which you can head to before or after your meal. Enjoy Filipino classic dishes like Pochero De Cebu and Crispy Pata with thirst quenchers like Buko Lychee shake on the side for a satisfying meal.
