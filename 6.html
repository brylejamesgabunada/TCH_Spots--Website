Charlie's cup

Pet lovers rejoice because there’s definitely a place you can stop at if you’re traveling with your furry friend! Charlie’s Cup is a treehouse-style cafe hanging by the cliffs of Balamban, treating you to a one-of-a-kind Busay mountain view. The fur-friendly cafe holds a special place for pets as it was named after the owner’s pet corgi. If you’re figuring out what to order, the cafe offers a wide variety of coffee drinks and a delectable all-day breakfast menu. We recommend pairing a cup of Americano with a filling order of Big Breakfast – made up of Spam, chorizo, two eggs, and rice. The cafe also has a small play area for kids to enjoy, a perfect rest stop for families exploring Busay.


  • Address: Transcentral Highway Sitio Inaad, Barangay, Cebu City
  • Opening Hours: 9:00AM - 6:30PM, Daily
