Serenity Mountain Cafe

For those looking for a getaway from the city, Serenity Mountain Cafe is a welcome break from the concrete buildings of the metro. One of the hidden gems nestled inside the Serenity Farm and Resort, the cafe offers visitors picturesque sights of Malubog and Sirao’s mountain skyline, which guests can enjoy over a hot cup of coffee and a variety of meals to choose from. Save room for dessert and have a taste of the newest addition to the menu – the popular Basque Burnt Cheesecake and Glutinous Mango Roll. If you schedule your visit at the end of the day and you’ll get a chance to see the cafe turn into a fairytale-like scene, with colorful string lights brightening up the cafe.


  • Address: Sitio Tiguib, Brgy. Malubog, Busay, Cebu City
  • Opening Hours: 9:00AM - 9:00PM, Daily
