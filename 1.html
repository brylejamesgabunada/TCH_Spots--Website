Fabio's Kitchen Pizza

A pizza, some bottles of beer, and the beauty of Busay’s mountains – who wouldn’t enjoy a break from the stress of the city at Fabio’s Pizza Kitchen? The cozy Italian restaurant treats guests to a great food selection and homey ambiance when they’re looking for a place to unwind at. The restaurant’s interior gives off a rustic feel – a sure photo opportunity for your Instagram feed. Order a side of their popular chicken wings along with your meal and no doubt your stomach will be filled!
